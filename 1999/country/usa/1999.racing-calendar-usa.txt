505 1999 USA Regatta Schedule
Draft - Last Updated 7 December, 1999
Current/Upcoming Season Events

We strive to list every 505 event in the USA (as well as some Canadian and other International events of interest) however we do not receive information on all events. You may also wish to check the Canadian 505 schedule, the International Schedule and contact a nearby fleet captain or regional coordinator to check for additional events.

Click on the highlighted contact name see e-mail address and phone numbers. Click on the highlighted event name to get a notice of race, or the regatta report. For changes and updates to this schedule, please contact Ali Meller

We try to keep this schedule accurate and up to date, but cannot guarantee it is. Always contact a regatta organizer to confirm events rather than simply relying on this schedule.
