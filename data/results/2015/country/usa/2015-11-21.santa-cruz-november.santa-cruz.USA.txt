Santa Cruz Nov 21st
Mike Holt
2015-11-23

All,

We had 4 boats out sailing on Saturday in gorgeous Fall conditions, we
sailed the first race in full hiking conditions, around 6 to 7 knots and
building with the boats starting to wire by the windward mark. Race one
was a short windward leeward race and at the finish it was a near photo
finish with Bruce and Rich crossing first ahead of Mike and Rob with Eb
and Geoff a hair behind them. 

Race 2 was turned around quickly and the wind had built into the teens.
We had a longer course, old style Olympic, Triangle, Sausage, Finish. We
were starting with the double handed boats so our start was quite busy
and picking a way through slower but higher boats was an interesting
challenge. A good first beat was followed by some fast downwind sailing
and with around 35 boats out racing the water was a congested area of
boats going in different directions and different speeds. By lap 2 the
race was taking shape, Rob and I led from Bruce and Rich, with Eb and
Geoff chasing them hard, Gordon and Segah a little further back. A fast
run followed, with Eb pushing Bruce all the way down and Bruce chasing
us. But the positions remained the same.

Race 3 was then sailed on a slightly shorter course, with the first beat
a tight affair, we just led at the top from Eb with Bruce sneaking
in-between at the top mark, we then had a fast 2 sail reach to the gybe
mark, not sure I can remember the last time I did that followed by what
was effectively a wire run to the leeward mark. In the traffic at the
bottom the wind lightened and for a while Eb had passed Bruce, but Bruce
was first to go square and pulled back through. Lap 2 was a busy affair
with big boats everywhere, but fun going past them at speed! Rob and
I managed to win again from Bruce and Rich, with Eb and Geoff again in
3rd.

A fantastic days sailing, warm, great breeze and a well run day. The
next one is December 19th, be there!
