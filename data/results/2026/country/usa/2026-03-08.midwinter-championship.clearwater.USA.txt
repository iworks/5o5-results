Starting 2025 off hot with Midwinters 🌴

Nine teams from the East Coast and Canada gathered in Clearwater, FL for
this year’s Midwinter Championship. An early-March schedule brought
welcome warmer weather—especially appreciated by northern sailors coming
off a particularly cold winter.

The regatta featured a consistent pattern of light easterlies shifting
toward the south and southwest as the sea breeze developed, and over
three days race committee were able to complete nine quality races. 

In the end, Ethan Bixby and Ted Huebner tied on points with Craig
Thompson and Adam Wolnikowski, but claimed the championship with more
race wins. Duane Delfosse and Mike Hull finished third overall, just two
points back. Barney Harris and Chris Gorton had a standout final day,
winning three of the four races.

Post-sailing debriefs on Days 1 and 2, led by Craig Thompson, sparked
valuable discussions on tactics, conditions, and boat setup—highlighting
the collaborative and teaching spirit of the East Coast 505 fleet.

Many thanks to Ethan Bixby for organizing the event, Donna Sue Marks for
traveling four hours to run the racing, and the friendly staff and
volunteers at Clearwater Community Sailing Center. Mark your calendars:
next year’s 505 Midwinters are already set for March 5–7 in Clearwater ‼️
