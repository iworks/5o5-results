Mike Martin's Long Beach report
Mike Holt
2014-02-19
SCYA Midwinters

The SCYA Midwinters regatta was held over the Feb 15 and 16 at ABYC in beautiful Long Beach, CA. Here are the highlights:

o 8 505s
o Rigging on the beach in shorts and t-shirts
o Dave Chatham returns to the 505 fleet with the purchase of 8786
o Ben Benjamin returns to the 505 fleet sailing with Mike Martin
o 4 races in 4 to14 knots of breeze on Saturday
o Ben and Mike lead at first mark every race on Sat but struggle downwind
o Andy and Howie School fleet with two 1st and two 2nd place finishes
o Reeve Dunne and Jeff Condon sail fast and smart to be in 2nd overall on Sat
o Howie has 61st birthday party on sat night with some very tasty Mexican food
o 3 races in 2-5 knots of breeze on Sunday
o Dan Downing  and Gary Lee show moments of great speed
o Andy and Howie School fleet with two 1st and a 2nd
o Rob Woelfel and Mike Holt have a 1,2,3  to tie Ben and Mike for 2nd  overall (but lose the tie breaker)
