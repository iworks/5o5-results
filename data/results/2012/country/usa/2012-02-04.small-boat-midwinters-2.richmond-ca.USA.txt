Small Boat Midwinters #2 - Richmond Yacht Club
Geoffrey Gales
2012-02-06

Hello 505 sailors,

This past weekend 9 teams showed up to race at the Small Boat Midwinters
at the Richmond Yacht Club. The wind was light, perhaps 2-6 knots, but
the racing was tight. The race committee was able to complete two and
a half races before the wind shut off for the day. 

Mike Holt and Rob Woelfel won the day with two bullets and a second;
followed by Bob Tennant and Rich Mundel who scored two seconds and
a first. 

The rest of the fleet could not (or would not) finish the third race so
the results for the day looked like this:

Mike Holt and Rob Woefel -1, 1, 2
Bob Tennant and Rich Mundel- 2,2,1
Bruce Edwards and  Dave "Tosser" Shelton- 5, 3, dnf
Hasso Plattner and Peter Alarie- 3, 5, dnf
Jeff Miller  and  Mike Smith- 4, 6, dnf
Geoff Gales and Julien Marcil -7, 4, dnf
Mr. O'Leary and Crew - 6, 8,dnf
Ryan Nelson and Crew -8, 7, dnf
Holt Condon and Crew dns, 9, dnf


This series will continue on Sunday, March 4. I'm guessing that all
9 boats will return next month in the hopes of catching some stronger
wind. They may also wish to return because they are all preregistered
--and prepaid for the day.  


But before that event many of these teams will head South for the
Alamitos Bay Yacht Club Midwinters (Feb 18-19) and then back to the Bay
for the St. Francis YC Spring Dinghy (March 10-11).

And then South again for the Pacific Coast Championships (March 16-18).
What a fantastic winter. 

We'll see everyone in 2 weeks!

Geoff Gales USA 8888
