Spring Dinghy Report
mail4mikemartin
2018-03-26

Spring has arrived and so has the Spring Dinghy ⛵️. We had a good
showing with 16 boats registered. All except 2 🍆 showed up and sailed.
Saturday served up some super tactical racing. At various times in the
first race, five different boats held the lead. As we all know, the only
thing that matters is who was leading when they  crossed the finish line
🏁. In this race it was Andy and Howie 🦆. The next 2 races the wind
💨 increased a bit to 12-15 but was equally shifty. Again lots of places
changed with the new team of Reeve Dunne and Michael Menninger, in the
recently refinished Black Boat, taking race 2 and Adam Lowry & Mike
Martin taking Race 3. In all races that day, whomever led at the first
mark did not win the race. 

Sunday the breeze was a bit steadier and stronger 🚩. With a solid ebb
tide, Adam and Mike took the first 2 races but had a swim 🏊🏻‍♂️,
letting Andy & Howie and Michael & Reeve battle it out to break their
tied scores. Reeve and Michael led at last leeward gate, but swam
🏊🏻‍♂️ on their final tack to let Andy and 🦆 take the race win and
2nd overall 🥈.Reeve and Michael took third🥉 overall with Adam and Mike
taking the regatta win🥇.

It was great to see 8680 under new ownership and on the water with Mark
Voropayev and Evan Diola who did a great job finishing on even points
with class veterans PVG and Kerry Poe. It was also good to see Eric
Anderson teaming up with Parker Shinn to duke it out with the top
competitors.  

The next event at StFYC  is the Elvstrom Zellerbach regatta April 28-28.
This will be the last chance to sail at the NAs venue before the big
event, so be sure to put it on your calendar, and come out and sail.
